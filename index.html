    Story

    The main goal for the project of this semester is to create visualization that supports the idea that hotels and motels are often used as venues for sex trafficking. The visualizations are meant to be able to prove that hotels should establish regulations that train workers in detecting sex trafficking and forcing them to help. There is a lack of clear information and data on the use of hotels and venues, databases that have this information are not publicly available therefore, our goal has been divided into different tasks all with the hope to build up visualizations on these topics.

    1. Create visualizations about the general topic of sex trafficking - COMPLETED
    2. Create our own database about sex trafficking with hotels as venues - IN PROGRESS
    3. Create visualizations regarding sex trafficking - IN PROGRESS

    Reflection

    Our communication worked very well. We had group messages that allowed us to quickly let others know what we have done, what needs to be done, what questions we had, and any reminders of deadlines. It was very difficult in the beginning to find data on lawsuits for hotels and child sex trafficking. Data and information was kept vague and under the radar. To combat this in the future, it would be a good idea to have backup plans for different situations from the beginning. It's good to remember to adapt and adjust, which will be useful in the real world. For example, we had to change our data objectives somewhat to be able to obtain data that could be used. Otherwise, had we stuck with just child sex trafficking and hotels, there would be nothing to work with. We also adjusted our original strategy from just reading case studies to focusing more on data, which is what are our strengths.
